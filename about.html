about



I am a multidisciplinary artist playing with the lines between the organic , and human-made imagery. I paint, sculpt, and draw but I am currently most focused on tattooing. I am very inspired by the world around me, mainly plants, rocks, the pattern of sunlight on water and roots curling through the earth. I am also heavily inspired by medieval and high fantasy imagery. I love the idea of using gargoyles, goblins and demons as protectors and guardians.

I work out of a private studio in east Oakland, my practice is centered around creating a safe space where people can play and experiment and grow into their own identity. My sessions are a collaborative experience where the voices and opinions of my clients are always prioritized and the final tattoo will be the product of continual adjustment and check-ins. My prices range from 200$ to 400$ depending on the size, complexity and placement of the design. I am also currently offering free tattoos for Black folks, as well as tattoos over self harm scars. When I'm not tattooing I love to swim in rivers, collect good sticks and rocks and play in the dirt.

I've been tattooing for seven years
I really really love tattoing and getting to talk to each of you!
